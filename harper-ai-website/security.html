Security FAQ

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored in AWS data centers in your selected region. We offer US, EU, and APAC regions with full data residency.

Can I bring my own encryption keys?

Yes, enterprise customers can use their own encryption keys through our BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) program.

How often do you conduct security audits?

We conduct third-party security audits annually, penetration testing quarterly, and continuous automated scanning.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your data before cancellation. We retain data for 30 days post-cancellation, then permanently delete it.
