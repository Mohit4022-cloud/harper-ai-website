Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes, we offer a 20% discount when you pay annually. Contact our sales team for more details.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your data before cancellation. We retain your data for 30 days after cancellation.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. Enterprise customers may opt for paid onboarding services.
